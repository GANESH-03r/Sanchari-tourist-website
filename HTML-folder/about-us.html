Our Story

Born from a love of travel across India

Sanchari was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate travelers who believed that every Indian deserves to experience the breathtaking diversity of their own country — from the snow-capped Himalayas to the tropical backwaters of Kerala.

The word "Sanchari" means "wanderer" in Sanskrit — and that is exactly who we are. We have spent years exploring hidden gems, iconic landmarks, and everything in between, so we can bring you the best curated travel experiences.

Today we help thousands of travelers every year discover the magic of India with carefully designed packages, expert local guides, and seamless end-to-end support.
